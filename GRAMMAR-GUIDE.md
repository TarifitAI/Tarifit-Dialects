# Tarifit Grammar Guide

This document provides a basic overview of the grammar used in the Tarifit AI project. The goal is to maintain consistency across all dialect datasets.

---

## 1. Word Order

The most common sentence order is:

**Subject – Verb – Object (SVO)**

Example:

- Aryaz yecča aghrum.
- The man ate bread.

Alternative word orders may occur depending on emphasis and dialect.

---

## 2. Nouns

Nouns have gender:

- Masculine
- Feminine

Examples:

| Masculine | Feminine |
|------------|-----------|
| aryaz | tamghart |
| argaz | tafat |

---

## 3. Plurals

Plural forms vary by dialect and should be documented individually.

Example:

| Singular | Plural |
|-----------|---------|
| aryaz | iryazen |

| English | Tarifit |
|-----------|----------|

## 5. Verbs

Each verb entry should include:

- Infinitive
- Present tense
- Past tense
- Imperative (if available)

Example:

| Meaning | Verb |
|----------|------|
| Eat | ečč |
| Drink | su |
| Go | ruḥ |
